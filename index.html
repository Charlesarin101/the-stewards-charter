THE THREE-PILLAR FOUNDATION

THE SHIELD

Protection

  • Life cover for family continuity
  • Personal accident support

THE ENGINE

Growth

  • Long-term savings & investment discipline
  • Education, home, business goals

THE BRIDGE

Control

  • Top-ups & policy loans (where applicable)
  • Structured withdrawals & annuity pathways
  • Minimum duration: 1 Year

WHO THIS IS FOR

Retirees & Those Planning Retirement

For people who want to convert savings or a lump sum into a guaranteed monthly income for life—protecting dignity and preventing outliving resources.

Focus: Annuity Plans (lifetime income & peace of mind).

Working Adults, Business Owners & Families

For people who want disciplined savings with life protection—building a personal fund for education, projects, and long-term wealth while protecting family continuity.

Focus: Traditional Plans (e.g., Esusu Shield).

In short: if someone is thinking about securing a stable retirement or building protected savings for family goals, they are a potential fit.
